Gravity Falls Journals: Secrets & Hidden Lore

Origin of the Journals

The three journals in Gravity Falls—Journals 1, 2, and 3—are packed with paranormal research, cryptic codes, and forbidden knowledge. Stanford Pines (Grunkle Stan's twin brother), a brilliant scientist, wrote them to catalog the supernatural phenomena in Gravity Falls before being pulled into another dimension. Originally for research, but later used as a survival guide and secret weapon against entities like Bill Cipher.

Journal Contents

Journal 1

Earliest anomalies Ford documented. Includes dimensional theories, first contact with Bill Cipher. Eventually stolen by Gideon.

Journal 2

Middle journal, contains blueprints and cryptid behavior. Ford used this for early expermentation. Hidden until the events of Season 2

Journal 3

Final journal, contains the most advanced research and the most dangerous secrets. Dipper finds this journal in the woods and uses it to fight Bill Cipher. Also Contains: Creature entries (gnomes, shapeshifter, etc.), Portal schematics, Cipher clues, Anti-Bill strategies, Warnings: “Trust No One.”
