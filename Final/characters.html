Meet the Characters

Gravity Falls is not just a show about mysteries; it's also about the characters who navigate those mysteries. Each character brings their own unique personality, quirks, and story arcs to the series. From the curious Dipper Pines to the enigmatic Bill Cipher, the characters are what make Gravity Falls truly special.

Character Profiles

  • Dipper Pines: The curious and intelligent twin who is determined to uncover the secrets of Gravity Falls.
  • Mabel Pines: Dipper's energetic twin sister, known for her optimism and love for adventure.
  • Grunkle Stan: The twins' great-uncle and the shady operator of the Mystery Shack.
  • Soos: The lovable handyman of the Mystery Shack, known for his goofy personality.
  • Wendy Corduroy: A cool teenager who works at the Mystery Shack and becomes a close friend to Dipper.
  • Bill Cipher: The enigmatic dream demon who serves as the main antagonist of the series.
  • Ford Pines: Dipper and Mabel's great-uncle, a brilliant scientist studying the mysteries of Gravity Falls.
